Users (Implemented)

Admin authentication table. Stores login credentials with username, password, and role fields. Secured via IPC bridge.

Students (Implemented)

Core registry with DS-ID, NIC, contact info, license class, and 8-stage pipeline tracking (stage 1-8). Primary entity.

Instructors (Proposed)

Driving trainer profiles with license number, specialization (Manual/Auto), and active status. Links to Lessons.

Vehicles (Proposed)

Fleet tracking with plate number, model, transmission type, and maintenance status. Links to Lessons.

Payments (Proposed)

Financial transactions: full payments, installments, registration fees. Tracks amount, method, date, and balance status.

Lessons (Proposed)

Training session scheduling. Links Student + Instructor + Vehicle. Tracks date, time, duration, type, and completion status.

Exams (Proposed)

Written and practical exam records with date, location, score, and pass/fail result per student.

Documents (Proposed)

Student document checklist: NIC, photos, medical certificates, birth certificates. Tracks verification status.
